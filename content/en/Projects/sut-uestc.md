## Hybrid Algorithms for NISQ Devices

Focused on constructing robust quantum algorithms specifically targeted at the limitations and capabilities of modern Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum hardware.

**Key Projects & Accomplishments:**
- Developed Variational Quantum Eigensolver (VQE) models via Qiskit and PennyLane to simulate the 2D Heisenberg Hamiltonian.
- Engineered classical-quantum workflows that optimized parametrized circuit architectures.
- Benchmarked and mitigated noise affecting ground-state energy approximations against classic exact diagonalization results.
