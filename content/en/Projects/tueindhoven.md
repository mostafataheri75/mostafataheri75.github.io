## Machine Learning for Fluid Dynamics

Pioneered the intersection of complex systems physics and modern Deep Learning by creating predictive models for granular flow.

**Key Projects & Accomplishments:**
- Trained large-scale deep learning models via Keras/TensorFlow for predicting non-linear dynamics of rotating granular mixtures.
- Integrated computer vision and time-series extraction to dynamically classify phase transitions in physical systems.
- Built data processing bridges directly from experimental physical setups into ingestible machine learning feature sets.
