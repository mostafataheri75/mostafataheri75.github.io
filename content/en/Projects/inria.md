## AI-Driven Mathematical Reasoning & Theorem Proving

- Implemented an LLM-powered evolutionary algorithm designed to autonomously generate optimization structures.
- Built high-throughput LLM pipelines on A100 GPUs using vLLM for models such as Qwen and Mistral.
